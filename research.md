# Research Program

I develop **data-driven, network-based methods** to understand molecular complexity in space.  
All datasets follow **FAIR principles**.

---

## 1. Chemical Network Science

- Network representations of astrochemical systems  
- Quantitative measures of molecular complexity  
- Reaction pathway analysis  

Enables comparison across:
- interstellar clouds  
- comets & asteroids  
- lab ice & gas experiments  

---

## 2. Data-Driven FAIR Analysis

- Automated molecular assignment pipelines  
- Ensemble molecular statistics  
- Network-based pattern recognition  

Supports reproducibility, cross-lab comparison, and long-term usability.

---

## 3. Applications

- Cometary/asteroid material (Rosetta, Hayabusa2)  
- Interstellar ice & gas  
- Planetary atmospheres  

---

## Long-Term Vision

Create an **Astrochemical Data Science Lab** integrating:  
laboratory astrochemistry, space missions, chemical network modeling, machine learning, and FAIR data.
