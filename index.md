## Research Focus

Develops **network-based, data-driven approaches** to astrochemical systems.

Studies **high-dimensional molecular ensembles** to quantify, compare, and predict chemical complexity.

FAIR and reproducible principles are central.

---

## Group & Environment

Leads an independent group at ORIGINS Cluster, collaborating with space missions, labs, and observatories.

---

## Long-Term Perspective

Aims to establish an **Astrochemical Data Science Laboratory** integrating:  
- laboratory astrochemistry  
- space-mission analytics  
- chemical network modeling  
- machine learning  
- FAIR data infrastructures
